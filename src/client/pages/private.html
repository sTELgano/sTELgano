How it works

You and one other person. Under a minute.

sTELgano connects two people who already trust each other. You'll need someone to share the number and pairing code with — a partner, a source, a friend.

Create it and share

Generate a number that matches their real one — local if theirs is local, the same country if it's international — and add it as a second number on their existing contact card, where it blends in. sTELgano also gives you a one-time pairing code: send it to them with the number. They need both to join.

Each pick a PIN

You each choose your own private PIN. It never leaves your device — it's hashed locally to prove you're allowed in.

Open the channel

Enter the number and your PIN — plus the one-time pairing code the first time you join. Your browser derives the key and connects you, end-to-end encrypted, nothing stored. Once you're both in, the channel seals.

What our servers never see

  • Your messages— encrypted in your browser with AES-256-GCM before they ever leave it.
  • The phone number— only an irreversible hash reaches us. We can't recover it.
  • Your PIN— hashed on your device, never transmitted or stored.
  • Any account— there isn't one. No email, no signup, no profile to sell and no data-broker trail. Nothing ties a session to you.

Built to protect against someone with physical access to your phone — a partner, a family member. We're honest about the limit: it is not designed to defeat a government or law enforcement.
